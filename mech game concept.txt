Text based, turn based Mech RPG

Multiple mech types, including Humanoid and Pony mechs

Managing multiple ressources to do actions - shields, generator, computing power, HP, Heat.

Mechs are customizable, and can equip modular parts, as long as they function well together (energy consumption, weight limits, number of slots for weapons and accessories, etc.) Multiple weapon types.

Adverse effects, such as EMP.

Different levels of attack with weapons

Command line and button controls
